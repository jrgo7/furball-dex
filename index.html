THE FURBALL DEX 2

The ordinary FURBALL is a penguin-duck combination, with a slight hint of cat. A real menace.

This noble FURBALL sells drugs in MERCURY DRUGSTORE to earn a living and support his children.

FURBALLs which have made it big in the business world through fair means or foul.
No.005 BEDTIME FURBALL CITY TYPE
Sleeps in the light, lives in the night, BEDTIME FURBALLs keep the world working right.

BEE FURBALLs cannot talk; instead, they use flying dances to communicate with each other.
No.007 APPLE FURBALL FOREST TYPE

No.008 WAR FURBALL FRONTIER TYPE

No.009 WI-FI FURBALL CITY TYPE

No.011 DEVIL FURBALL MYTHICAL TYPE

No.012 JUDGE FURBALL MYTHICAL TYPE
The father of ANGEL and DEVIL FURBALLs casts judgements in criminal trials.
No.013 STEAMPUNK FURBALL FRONTIER TYPE
The INDUSTRIAL REVOLUTION inspired STEAMPUNK FURBALLs to use steam in unique ways.
No.014 DIRTBALL FRONTIER TYPE
These smaller-than-usual FURBALLs plan ambushes from dirt holes at the FUR-TANOOK FRONTIER.
No.015 BUSY FURBALL CITY TYPE
Always busy and on the phone, BUSY FURBALLs hope to become a RICH FURBALL sooner or later.
